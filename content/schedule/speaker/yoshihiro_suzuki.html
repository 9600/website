Bio

Last year, Yoshihiro Suzuki started his own business (D3 Communications) which currently provides realtime service architechture planning and design. And he also founded and leads RSi Project (Realtime Service Initiative Project) in Japan which is research / develop / deploy project for realtime services based on XMPP protocol suites. In last decade, his research topic are access network architectures and services in FTTH and 4G mobile network at Panasonic.
