Bio

Thomas is one of the core members of MuseScore. He mainly focuses on managing the project and evangelizing MuseScore on events such as the Music Hack Days. Besides taking care of the internationalization aspect of MuseScore, he is also developing and maintaining the musescore.org and musescore.com website. Besides MuseScore, he is also engaged in Drupal since 2004.
