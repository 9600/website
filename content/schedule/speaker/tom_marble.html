Bio

Tom Marble formerly worked at Sun Microsystems as an Enterprise Systems Engineer, then progressed to Chief Technologist for Sun's national dot.com sales area before joining R&D as a Senior Java Performance Engineer. Following Sun's decision to open source the Java programming language he was selected to be the first to hold the role of OpenJDK Ambassador.

Most recently he has launched Informatique, Inc.: a consultancy to leverage his electrical engineering background along with his software development experience for clients in such domains as telematics for electric vehicles, smart grid interoperability, probabalistic model checking, autonomous cyber defense, multiplayer online gaming and highly concurrent software design.

Tom is an enthusiastic open source software contributor and specializes in the Debian GNU/Linux distribution.

For FOSDEM 2012 Tom is a manager for the Free Java and Legal Issues DevRooms.
