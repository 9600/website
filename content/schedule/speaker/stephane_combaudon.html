Bio

Stéphane is a MySQL DBA at Dailymotion, one of the leading video-sharing websites in the world. He has been using the LAMP platform for almost 10 years in various companies and is particularly enthusiastic about MySQL. He is a regular speaker at conferences on MySQL and on free software.
