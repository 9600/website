Bio

Kostas lives in Greece, works as a firefighter and has been contributing to the openSUSE distribution for a few years now, mainly in the domains of marketing and promotion, as well as being an openSUSE Ambassador for Greece. He has organized several openSUSE release and hacking parties all over Greece and is currently in charge of and working on a complete overhaul of the openSUSE ambassador program. He has a lot of energy, enthousiasm, and loves to sharewith openSUSE and other FOSS projects, as well as quite some experience in running and coordinating local communities.
