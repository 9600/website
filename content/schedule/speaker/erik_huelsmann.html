Bio

Long time FOSS contributor as full committer for Subversion, LedgerSMB, ArmedBear Common Lisp and a number of smaller projects as well as contributions to numerous projects.

Erik Huelsmann is a long time Subversion committer and the original designer of the WC-NG new working copy format in Subversion 1.7 as well as being a major contributor in several other projects (LedgerSMB, ArmedBear Common Lisp. With current business activities ranging from being one of the driving forces behind the Tech Coop (http://tech.coop/) hosting business [a hosting service specialized at running webapplications written in Common Lisp] to being a business and information analyst in both logistics and financial services industries, Erik brings with him a wide range of business experiences.
