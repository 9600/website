Bio

In the last 5 years Peitro has been involved with two academic research projects, Cduce and Mancoosi founded by European community. Recently he has joined the center for research and innovation on Free Software. He is a strong supporter of the Debian project (despite he never officially joined it).

His current academic interests are software engineering, theorem proving, functional programming, modal logic. His focus at the moment is on open source and in particular in relation to quality assurance aspects of software distribution and component based system.
