Bio

Alejandro, who holds a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of A Coru単a, has been working at Igalia since 2006. He is involved in the development of projects using GTK+/GNOME technologies since then. He has been involved in GNOME accessibility technologies since 2007. Right now he is the co-maintainer of ATK (Accessibility Toolkit), former maintainer of Hail (Hildon ATK implementation) and implemented ATK for Clutter, GNOME Shell and Unity. He also participates in the GNOME Build Brigade, and provided some fixes for GAIL (GNOME Accessibility Implementation Library) and other GNOME projects.
