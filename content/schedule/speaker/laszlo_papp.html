Bio

Laszlo Papp (djszapi) graduated in electric engineering. He is a 26 year old Senior Software Engineer currently employed by Symbio Software Oy in Helsinki, Finland. He has been working on the Maemo/MeeGo project with a genuine interest in smart embedded systems. He had been working as a Linux kernel developer for years. He has been a Qt/KDE enthusiast in his leisure time for while, visiting opensource Qt/KDE events and sprints as much as possible all over the world. He likes reading interesting technical books, playing bass guitar and visiting opensource local community meetups (MeeGo, Qt, Igda) in order to share ideas. He is also one of the developers behind the Gluon project which is a free and opensource game development and distribution platform.
