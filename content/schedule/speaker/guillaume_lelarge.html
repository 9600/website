Bio

Guillaume Lelarge is involved with the PostgreSQL community as a contributor, and as an evangelist. He developed many patches for PostgreSQL, pgAdmin, pgpool, checkpostgres, Slony, etc. He's even a commiter on pgAdmin, pgpool, and checkpostgres. As an evangelist, he wrote many articles in french computer magazines, translated the PostgreSQL manual, and Greg Smith's book, and gave many talks in France. He's also vice-treasurer in PostgreSQL Europe.

At work, he's the CTO of Dalibo, a french company highly specialized on PostgreSQL. He's often on customer's workplace to provide support and training.
