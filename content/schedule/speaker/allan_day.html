Bio

Allan Day is a long-standing member of the GNOME design team, and has worked on the design of many GNOME components, including GNOME Shell, the GNOME Control Center, Nautilus and Contacts. He is also a member of the GNOME marketing team. He works for Red Hat as a User Experience designer.
