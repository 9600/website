Bio

Jean Pihet is working with embedded Linux since many years now, for companies like Texas Instruments, MontaVista, Motorola and Philips. Recently NewOldBits.com has been founded to provide high quality consulting services. The area of work is mainly OMAP Power Management, tracing and profiling tools (perf, ftrace, oprofile...) for recent ARM cores.
