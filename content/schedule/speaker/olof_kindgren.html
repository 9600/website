Bio

Olof is working for ORSoC, owner and maintainer of opencores.org, as an FPGA designer with a focus on Open Source Hardware. He has a MScEE in Integrated Electronic System Design and many years of experience in building and verifying complex and flexible hardware solutions. On the software side he has contributed to various Open Source projects over the years, and is now mainly focused on improving the OpenCores ecosystem around the OpenRISC CPU
