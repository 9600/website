Bio

Jukka has worked with Nokia on Symbian, maemo and MeeGo products. Most recently he was the product & community manager for MeeGo Community Edition for N900/N950. Currently he is member of steering group for Nemo Mobile, that is continuing the work of MeeGo Community Edition.
