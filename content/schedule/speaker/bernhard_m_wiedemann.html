Bio

Bernhard M. Wiedemann is a long-time free software enthusiast and software developer. During the last decade he contributed to coreboot, qemu, VirtualBox, openSUSE and other projects. Having joined the openSUSE Testing Core Team in mid-2009, he started writing OS-autoinst (an automated testsuite for whole operating-systems) in April 2010 that has since helped to discover dozens of bugs. He is now employed by SUSE LINUX Products GmbH.
