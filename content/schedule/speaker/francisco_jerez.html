Bio

After his unsuccessful experience as a student and political activist in the last few years, Francisco Jerez got involved with the development of the reverse-engineered Nouveau driver and some of its surrounding parts in the Linux graphics stack. Right now he's doing his best to get Gallium3D's GPGPU support to finally take off.
