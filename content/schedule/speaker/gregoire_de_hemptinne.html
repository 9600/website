Bio

Gregoire is an Open-Source enthusiast and is involved in several projects promoting Free Software and Free Culture since a while. He studied computer engineering at UCL (Belgium) and is now software architect at Euranova. He essentially works on Eclipse RCP/EMF technologies and in particular on the Wazaabi open-source project. In his free time he is also the webmaster of the planet-libre.org project.
