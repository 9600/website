Bio

Nick Perez has been an active Perl community participant since 2003. He maintains several modules on CPAN and is a fan of interesting tech including the marriage of POE and Moose. Only recently moving to Amsterdam, NL, Nick lived in the US prior and spoke at a number of Perl events there including YAPC::NA, Perl Oasis, and Frozen Perl.
