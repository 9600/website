Bio

Richard "RichiH" Hartmann is a member of the board of the PDPC, freenode staff, and OFTC staff. His dayjob being a project and backbone management at Globalways AG, many of his nights are spent around FLOSS in general. His main interests hover around command line tools, data integrity, FLOSS communities, cold countries, and scuba diving.

He can be reached as RichiH on freenode, OFTC, and IRCnet; or by sending email to richih.mailinglist@gmail.com.
