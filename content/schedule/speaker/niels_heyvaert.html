Bio

Niels is GNU/Linux and FOSS enthousiast since 1996. At that time he was involved in embedded development projects. He's now involved in managing the stable Android ports to the Freerunner which he also presented in the Openmoko Devroom during FOSDEM 2010.Professionally, he's currently active as a freelance consultant.
