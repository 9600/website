Bio

Niibe is a contributer to GNU Project since 1989 and a Debian developer since 2005. He is one of founders and the chairman of Free Software Initiative of Japan. He is a long time GNU/Linux developer and the principal author of Gnuk, and the principal designer of FST-01 (Minimum STM32F103 USB computer).
