Bio

Andreas Granig is a member of the management board of Kamailio, the most popular open source SIP routing engine. He works with VoIP since 2000 and with SIP since 2004. Andreas is co-founder and CTO of Sipwise GmbH, a company based in Vienna/Austria developing and integrating carrier-grade open source VoIP systems serving large scale deployments.
