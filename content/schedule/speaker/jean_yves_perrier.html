Bio

Busy around the web since its infancy, he wrote his first Web site in 1994. He participated later in the Open Directory Project and is a former admin of the French Wikipedia. He's now working as Tech Writer on the Mozilla Developer Network, where he has been writing open web documentation since 2010.
