Bio

Francisco is studying the computer security Master in Chalmers in Sweden and doing his project to get the Computer Engineer degree in the UPV in Spain. He is also a Gentoo staffer (without access to the portage tree) where he does bug handling, assembler PIC fixing, doc writing, PR and evangelizing for the Gentoo Hardened project.

He is also known for mounting Direct Connect servers at various lan parties in Spain and Sweden, including Campus Party Valencia, and for being the network responsible at the Infor & Games Festival in Castellon. He also was a developer of the now unmaintained Kontinuidad Jabata project, where he adquired his fear of QT.
