Bio

Andrew Overholt works at Red Hat's Toronto, Canada office. In the past he has worked on PostgreSQL-related tools and the GNU Classpath and gcj projects. For the past 6 years he has been involved in Eclipse, both upstream and in packaging efforts in Fedora and other Linux distributions. He helps lead the Eclipse Linux Tools project at eclipse.org.
