Bio

Ronny Trommer is working since over 5 years in IT services and using OpenNMS since 2004. He is a contract lecturer in the University of Applied Sciences in Fulda, Germany for computer communication and distributed systems. He is lucky for the possibility to write a German OpenNMS book with his two friends Alexander Finger and Klaus Thielking-Riechert and is a member of the Order of the Green Polo since 2009. Since 2011 he is working at the University of Applied Sciences in Fulda and is a freelance developer and consultant around OpenNMS.
