Bio

In 2008, Christian co-founded Genode Labs, the company behind the Genode OS project. Before then, he finished his diploma thesis and conducted research of microkernel-based operating systems in the OS group of TU Dresden. Christian's mission is to push Genode to the OS for day-to-day usage.
