Bio

Thierry Carrez is the Release Manager for the Openstack project. An Ubuntu Core developer and Debian maintainer, he was previously the Technical lead for Ubuntu Server at Canonical, and an operational manager for the Gentoo Security Team. In a parallel life, he used to be an IT Manager for small and large companies. He is working home-based from a small village in the center of France, where he lives with his wife and two daughters.
