Bio

Jon Nordby is a software developer at Openismus GmbH, specializing in software development based on open technologies. The last year he has worked as part of the Nokia N9 development team, helping create its widely praised virtual keyboard. Jon holds a Bachelor degree in Electronics Engineering, and spends his spare time obsessing about peculiar music.
