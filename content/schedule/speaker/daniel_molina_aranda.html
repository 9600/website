Bio

Daniel Molina Aranda holds M.E. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science. He is now part of the OpenNebula team as project engineer, being a major contributor in its open-source community. His interests are in cloud and virtualization technologies. He has been involved in several EU-funded flagship projects in cloud computing, like RESERVOIR and NUBA and is now collaborating in StratusLab.
