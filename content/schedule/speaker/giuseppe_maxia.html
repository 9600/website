Bio

Giuseppe is the QA Director at Continuent. He is active member of the MySQL community and long timer open source enthusiast. During the past 25 years he has worked in various IT related fields, with focus on databases, object oriented programming, system administration. Fluent in Italian, English, Perl, Python, SQL, Lua, C, Bash, and good speaker of C , French, Spanish, Java. Giuseppe works in cyberspace, with a virtual team.
