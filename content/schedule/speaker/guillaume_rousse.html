Bio

Guilaume works as a security engineer in the French National Computer-Science Institute (INRIA). He is a long time member of Paris.pm, contributor of Mandriva, now Mageia Linux distribution, and regular writer for Linux Magazine. He uses Perl for various purposes, ranging from generic system administration to security assessment. His involvement in the FusionInventory project started with a few patches to almost completely rewriting the agent internals.
