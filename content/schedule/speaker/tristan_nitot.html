Bio

Tristan Nitot has co-founded Mozilla Europe in 2003 and has been its president until it merged with subsidiaries of Mozilla Foundation in 2011. Under his tenure, Mozilla has reached almost half a billion instances in the world, 45% being in Europe, where Community participation is also the highest.

Now Tristan is Principal Mozilla Evangelist, a worldwide position, where he tirelessly puts Mozilla's mission and non profit nature in perspective with Mozilla technologies and initiatives, explaining how user sovereignty is key for the future society that is being built.
