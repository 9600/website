Bio

Karanbir Singh is a CentOS Team member and Open Source Developer by night, Sysadmin by day. He lives and works in London, but prefers Belgian beers. He enjoys talking to people about how they use technology to make their lives easier and how open source has improved the general state of computing in the world. You can engage with him at http://www.karan.org/ or twitter.com/kbsingh
