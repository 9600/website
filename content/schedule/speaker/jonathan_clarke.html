Bio

Jonathan has been working on Open Source infrastructures since his beginnings as a system administrator. His work is now focused around configuration management tools, in particular Rudder. He is also a contributor to several open source projects including CFEngine, LSC and OpenLDAP. In his spare time, he enjoys cocktails, good food, cinema and cycling around Paris.
