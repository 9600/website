Bio

Nobuo Ogashiwa is an Associated Professor of the Faculty of International Social Studies, Maebashi Kyoai Gakuen College and is a Director of ICT Center of Maebashi Kyoai Gakuen College. His current research topic is mobile network technologies in college campus and XMPP software development (URL: http://github.com/ogashiwa/xmppd). He is a member of the RSi Project which is a XMPP-based Realtime Service project in Japan. And he is also a member of the WIDE Project which is a japanese initiatively internet research project.
