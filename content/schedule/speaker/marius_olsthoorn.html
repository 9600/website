Bio

Marius Olsthoorn is a software developer for 2dehands/2ememain, the market leader for classified ads in Belgium. 2dehands/2ememain is a website that uses an open-source stack with tools such as Perl, Moose, Catalyst, Apache, Memcached, Varnish, and MySQL.
