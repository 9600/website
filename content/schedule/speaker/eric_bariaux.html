Bio

Eric is a Java and iOS developer as well as Home Automation and Home Theater specialist, always looking to push the boundaries. As part of the Open Remote project, he draws on its practical experience in the automation field and knowledge of enterprise technologies to help design a truly open and modern platform that will unite a currently fragmented market and bring it into the "cloud and internet of things" age. In a previous life he has acted as senior developer, architect or project manager for enterprise J2EE projects in the financial, banking and telecommunication sector.
