Bio

Giovanni Battista Gallus is an attorney with more than ten years of practice in Internet and privacy law. He is a member (and former vice-president) of CGT, the Association of Cyberlawyers (Italy) and a senior partner in the law firm "Studio legale Gallus". Since 1999, he has devoted a substantial part of his practice to the legal implications of FLOSS and civil liberties on the Internet.
