Bio

I'm a web developer and partner of Igalia, where I've been hacking on LibrePlan since its early beginnings. As a member of the LibrePlan community, I strive everyday to build the best free software project management tool in the world and bring to an end the dominance of proprietary tools in the project-management landscape.

As an ordinary geek I like free software, the web and sharing.
