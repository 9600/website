Bio

Klaus is the CEO of LPI Central Europe, the master affiliate of the Linux Professional Institute in in Austria, Belgium, Czech Republic, Germany, the Netherlands, Luxemburg, Poland and Switzerland. He is also on the board of LPI e.V., the sub affiliate for the D-A-CH region. Since 1998, he works in the field of IT education and holds an LPI Level 1 Certification.
