Bio

Stéphane Magnenat is senior researcher at the Autonomous Systems Lab in ETH Zurich. He received the M.Sc degree in computer science (2003) and the Ph.D. degree (2010) from École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). His research interests include software architecture, software integration and scalable artificial intelligence on mobile robots.
