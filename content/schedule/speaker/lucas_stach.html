Bio

Lucas is a student at University of Applied Sciences Leipzig working on various free software project in his free time since 2007. He is deeply obsessed with understanding complex hardware well and assurance of quality of code. He had his first real contact with nouveau in 2009.

As of now Lucas is working together with the maintainers of the HWMON subsystem to allow proper usage of this framework within the Kernel to aid GPU drivers with temperature and fan management. Additionally he tries to bash the Gallium driver for the GeForce 5-7 series in shape.
