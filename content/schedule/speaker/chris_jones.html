Bio

Chris Jones is a member of Mozilla Research, and co-created the pdf.js and Boot2Gecko projects. Before joining Mozilla Research, Chris was a member of Mozilla's Platform Team, helping design Gecko's multi-process architecture (among other things). Chris joined Mozilla after a brief time as a graduate student in computer science at the University of California, Berkeley.
