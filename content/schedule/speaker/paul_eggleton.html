Bio

Paul Eggleton is an embedded Linux engineer working for Intel's Open Source Technology Centre. A long time member of the OpenEmbedded community, Paul joined Intel in November 2010 to work on the Yocto Project, concentrating mostly on core build system enhancements. Paul has been a software developer for 10 years and a contributor to a number of Open Source projects.
