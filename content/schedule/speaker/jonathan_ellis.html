Bio

Jonathan is Project Chair of Apache Cassandra and CTO and co-founder of DataStax. Prior to his work on Cassandra, Jonathan built a multi-petabyte, scalable storage system based on Reed-Solomon encoding for backup provider Mozy. Jonathan has spoken at conferences including JavaOne, OSCON, Strata, Devoxx, and PyCon.
