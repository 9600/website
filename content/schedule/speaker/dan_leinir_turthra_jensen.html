Bio

Known to most people as leinir, Dan is a software developer with the German-based KO GmbH, who has been involved with the KDE community for a number of years. First through Amarok, and more recently through the Gluon game creation and distribution framework, and it's GamingFreedom.org social gaming platform. Most recently, of course, he joined the Calligra project as a result of working for KO GmbH, whose prime area of expertise is exactly that. He currently resides in England, and is engaged to be married in May of this year, to his partner through several years.
