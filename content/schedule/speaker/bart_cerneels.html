Bio

Bart "Stecchino" Cerneels is a long time Amarok contributor that has recently made it to the Authors list. Despite being honored with that title he still has to work for a living as a freelance software architect. In his spare time he keeps on finding stuff to do in his eternally unfinished self-build solar passive zero-energy house and taking care of 2 cats and a fiancee.
