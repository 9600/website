Bio

Mike Milinkovich is the Executive Director of the Eclipse Foundation, responsible for supporting the Eclipse open-source community and commercial ecosystem. Mike is also a member of both the OpenJDK Board, and the JCP Executive Committee. In past lives he's worked for a number of startups such as Object Technology International (OTI), The Object People, and WebGain, as well as a number of large corporations such as Nortel, IBM and Oracle. He lives in Ottawa, Canada.
