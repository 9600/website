Bio

Alexander Neundorf is the maintainer of the CMake-based buildsystem of KDE4 and distributing to KDE since more than 10 years. Since 2006 he is part of the CMake team. In his day job he is developing seismic processing and analysis software for HPC Linux clusters at the Fraunhofer ITWM in Kaiserslautern.
