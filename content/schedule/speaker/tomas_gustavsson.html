Bio

Thomas is the project founder/manager of the EJBCA PKI project and CTO at PrimeKey. Long time developer and consultant with PKI/security solutions (nowadays exclusively for EJBCA and SignServer). Contributor to projects such as BouncyCastle (http://bouncycastle.org/). Technical lead for the development of a Common Criteria certified security library, CESeCore.
