Bio

Merlijn Hofstra has been working on various open source projects for nearly 10 years. In recent years he's been involved a lot with Xonotic and it's predecessor Nexuiz. Currently he has a leading role in the Xonotic core team, and mostly works on PR, legal and community related matters.
