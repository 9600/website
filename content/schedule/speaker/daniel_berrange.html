Bio

Daniel Berrange has been one of the lead developers & architects in the libvirt development community for the past 5 years. He is also the founder of the GTK-VNC, virt-manager, Test-AutoBuild and Entangle Photo projects, a significant contributor to the Xen, KVM, oVirt and SPICE-GTK projects, and maintainer of a number of Perl modules on CPAN. Daniel is a firm believer in the development and use of open source software under copyleft licenses.
