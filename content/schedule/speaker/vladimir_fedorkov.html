Bio

Vladimir Fedorkov is a Director of Support and Consulting at Sphinx Technologies. In his day-to-day work he's helping people to grow their websites by scaling and optimizing them and eliminating performance bottlenecks on a different levels. In the past he was worked with high-performance systems as a developer and team leader. Before joining Sphinx he was worked at Percona. In off-duty time he's enjoying travel, bicycling and photography.
