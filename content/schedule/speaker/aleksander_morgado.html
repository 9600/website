Bio

Aleksander Morgado is a Spanish Telecommunications Engineer with experience in several areas, such as Antivirus & Network Security, Satellite Orbit Determination systems and VoIP servers. He currently spends his work and free time among several Free Software projects, including GNU PDF, Gnome Tracker and NetworkManager/ModemManager.
