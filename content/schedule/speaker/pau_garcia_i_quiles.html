Bio

Pau is a software developer at Arisnova, a Spanish company specialized in Linux and Windows software development for the supervision and control (SCADA) of industrial environments. His dayjob goes from implementing embedded virtualization in a SCADA in C# to developing control software for battleships in C++, and of course, porting software from Windows to Linux and viceversa. In his spare time, he is a Debian and KDE developer and has a few open source projects of his own. Pau loves cinema, swimming and having a beer with friends.
