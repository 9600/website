Bio

Robert works as an open source developer in the Intel OTC Visualization team. He maintains the Cogl 3D graphics library and is part of the Intel Wayland development team. He also helps maintain Clutter and has a fair amount of window manager and graphics driver development experience. Before working at Intel, Robert worked at OpenedHand on Clutter and Cogl and before that worked at Imagination Technologies in the Linux OpenGL driver team.
