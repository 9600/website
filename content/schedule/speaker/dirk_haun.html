Bio

Open Source enthusiast with an interest in presenting, learning, and continuous improvement.

Dirk Haun has been a developer for smart card POS terminals, PDAs and smartphones, service level management tools, and systems and services for document processing and conversion. He has since moved on to the field of software quality assurance and build management.

In his spare time, Dirk is the maintainer of Geeklog, an open source CMS, which is probably best known for powering Groklaw.

Dirk is a frequent speaker at conferences and tech events, including FrOSCon and ACCU.
