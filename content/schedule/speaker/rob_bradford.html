Bio

Rob Bradford is a software engineer at the Intel Open Source Technology Centre based in London and previously part of Opened Hand. He has worked on all sorts of free software projects including various GNOME projects, Moblin and MeeGo. He now works in the area of graphics and is working on getting Wayland ready to rock the world.
