Bio

Brian King has been involved in the Mozilla community since 1999. In that time, he has worked building software on top of the Mozilla Application Platform. He is one of the Board of Directors of the Mozdev Community Organization, aka mozdev.org, a community site for hosting Mozilla-based projects and for promoting Mozilla technologies. Brian is a software consultant and head of Briks Software.
