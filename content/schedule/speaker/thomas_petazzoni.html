Bio

Since 2008, Thomas Petazzoni is an embedded Linux engineer and trainer at Free Electrons. With more than 800 patches merged into Buildroot since early 2009, he is the second most important contributor to the project, after the Buildroot maintainer himself. Thomas has also helped many Free Electrons customers in using Buildroot for various industrial products.
