Bio

Martijn Verburg (CTO - TeamSparq) has over 10 years experience as a technology professional and OSS mentor in a variety of environments from start-ups to large enterprises. He is the leader of the London Java User Group (LJC), and leads the global effort of JUG members who contribute to JSRs and the OpenJDK. Martijn's first book "The Well-Grounded Java Developer" with Ben Evans is being published by Manning. As a leading expert on technical team optimisation, his talks and presentations are in high demand by major conferences (JavaOne, Devoxx, OSCON, etc) where he is known for challenging the industry status quo as the "Diabolical Developer".
