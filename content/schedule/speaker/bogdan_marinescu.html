Bio

Bogdan Marinescu is a 33-years old software engineer from Bucharest (Romania) with many years of experience in software and hardware development. He started the eLua project in 2008 and has been actively involved in developing eLua and building a community around it since then. He is also the author of eLuaBrain and other devices built around eLua.
