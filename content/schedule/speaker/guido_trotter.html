Bio

Guido Trotter is a long time contributor in the FLOSS movement. A Debian Developer since 2002 and since 2005 working on virtualization-related projects. In 2006 he joined Google and there became a core developer on the Ganeti project, focusing on issues like scalability, concurrent job processing, and deployment infrastructure design. He has been presenting his work at many conferences, including LinuxDay, Debconf, LISA, FOSDEM (in the lightning and virtualization tracks) and LinuxCon with talks on Virtualization, Ganeti and advanced linux networking.
