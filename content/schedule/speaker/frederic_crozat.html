Bio

Frédéric Crozat has been involved in GNOME and Linux distribution for 11 years, first as GNOME packager for a Linux distribution formely knowned as Linux Mandrake, where he patched probably all GNOME components one time or another, then since 2002, as a member of GNOME Release Team. He also worked on speedboot feature on Mandriva Linux. During GNOME 3 development, he created a GNOME 3 live image to help people test GNOME 3 before it was released. Since mid 2010, he is working at SUSE on various stuff like SUSE MeeGo, lxc and more recently systemd.
