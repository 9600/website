Bio

Federico, Senior Software Engineer at Red Hat, is part of the VDSM team and involved in the oVirt/RHEV project. He often contributes to a wide range of open source components related to virtualization and storage systems. In his previous position, he worked for 6 years in an Italian startup contributing to open source and developing projects including a business continuity solution based on RHCS and KVM.
