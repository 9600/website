Bio

Ian Sullivan lives in Brooklyn and works at the Software Freedom Law Center. He is one of two leaders of the Wikiotics project, building colloborative tools for interactive language instruction. He is also the lead designer for the BookLiberator DIY book scanning project and helps coordinate the various FreedomBox efforts started at FOSDEM11.
