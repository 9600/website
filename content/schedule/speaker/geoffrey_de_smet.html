Bio

Geoffrey De Smet is the Drools Planner lead and works for Red Hat. He started programming Java applets in 2000 and graduated in 2003. He’s contributed to many open source projects (such as spring-richclient and maven plugins). In 2006, he founded Drools Planner and it’s his passion ever since. He joined Red Hat in 2010.
