Bio

Pierre-Yves Ricau is the technical leader of the AndroidAnnotations project. As a Java consultant, he had experiences with good old JEE development as well as Android app coding, and now tries to bring industry standards into Android development. He now works for Siine Ltd on a revolutionary Android keyboard.
