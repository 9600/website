Bio

Julian Stecklina finished his Diplom (Master's thesis) in 2009 at the Operating Systems Group of TU Dresden, in which he implemented a Firewire-based remote kernel debugger for NOVA that can be quickly retargeted to other kernels. During this time he became acquainted with the two original NOVA authors (one was his thesis advisor) and afterwards started to work on improving network I/O virtualization in NOVA as a PhD student.
