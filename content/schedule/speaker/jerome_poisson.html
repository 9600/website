Bio

Developer and traveller, I have worked on many more or less small projects (Saitek P2500 kernel driver, lm, mkvconvert, gcp, etc), but "Salut à Toi" is definitely my most important project. Politically engaged, I'm mainly developing the "Salut à Toi" project to have a libre, decentralised and privacy-friendly way to communicate. I'm currently working at Substantiel in Paris, a society developing "Ordissimo", an easy to use computer based on Debian and EFL.
