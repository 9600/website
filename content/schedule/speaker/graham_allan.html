Bio

Graham graduated from the University of Strathclyde with a BSc Hons in Software Engineering in 2010. Since then he has worked in the finance industry with TIM Group, based in London, UK.

Graham has a particular interest in useful and practical static analysis. Mutability Detector is his most significant contribution to open source Java, but he has also released some FindBugs plugins and utilities which are in active use at TIM Group. Recently, Graham has increased his interest in the OpenJDK, taking part in the JDK8 Warnings Cleanup Day, and signing up to assist in the development of JSR-353 Java API for JSON Processing.
