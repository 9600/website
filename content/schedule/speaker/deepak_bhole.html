Bio

Deepak Bhole is a Principal Software Engineer at Red Hat. He has been involved with OpenJDK and IcedTea since 2008 and is the maintainer of IcedTea-Web. As of September 2011, he has also taken on the role of Engineering Manager for the Java group at Red Hat.
