Bio

Oliver Burger was a long time contributor to Mandriva as German translator and as packager and moderator of the German mandriva user community mandrivaUser.de. He joined the Mageia project on day 1 in september 2010 and became an active member of the project.

He is now - as leader of the Mageia i18n team - a member of the Mageia council. He is also contributing to Mageia as a packager and in the documentation and web teams.
