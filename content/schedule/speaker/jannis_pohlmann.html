Bio

Jannis is one of the developers behind the Xfce desktop environment. Today, he is mainly responsible for maintaining and improving the Thunar file manager and the Tumbler thumbnailing service. Jannis recently graduated from University and has joined Codethink as a software developer at their offices in Manchester. Aside from hacking, he spends most of his spare time on making music in bands or solo projects.
