Bio

David Edmundson is a PhD student at Loughborough University, and has been active contributor to KDE for the last 4 years. David first became active in KDE Telepathy 18 months ago, and has spent the last year leading the KDE Telepathy project taking it through its first releases up to the stage it is now as a real usable product.
