Mobile site

    http://fosdem.org/mobile/

The online mobile schedule uses jQueryMobile 1.0a2 and hence supports all the platforms jQM does.

It is pretty hefty (a bit over 600kb in size) but includes everything when fetching that single URL, except the details about speakers and talks that are downloaded from the m.fosdem.org site when consulted.
