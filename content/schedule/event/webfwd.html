WebFWD ("Web Forward") [https://webfwd.org/] is Mozilla's Open Innovation program. We support Open Source projects which extend the Web; providing participants with mentorship from industry experts, access to the Mozilla global network, infrastructure and other world-class resources.

This talk will give some more detail on what types of projects fit in the program, and how you can apply. Innovate with is and help move the Web Forward.
