The presentation will describe the state of the OpenNebula project for data center virtualization, with a focus on the different innovative projects and infrastructures using OpenNebula as a platform for innovation and interoperability. We will show how OpenNebula integrates with other projects in the cloud and virtualization ecosystem and the potential areas for collaboration.

  • Daniel Molina Aranda
