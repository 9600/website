Getting to the point of having a working, low-resource DLNA-ready implementation is a hard task. Fortunately there's an easy way out - Rygel. It is a modular and extensible solution to help you out of that dilemma with minimum effort.

Rygel started its life for GNOME Mobile and was the designated UPnP/DLNA stack for Maemo Freemantle and MeeGo/Harmattan. It is supposed to work out-of-the box with no setup required, operating nicely with all kinds of quirky devices.
