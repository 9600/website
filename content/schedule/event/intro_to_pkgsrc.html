Track: BSD Devroom

pkgsrc is a cross systems packaging system, used in NetBSD, but also DragonflyBSD, MirBSD, Minix3 and a lot more. Through the example of shotwell, a digital pictures management application, this talk will give an introduction to pkgsrc for users and packagers.

Speakers

  • Guillaume Lasmayous
