--- title: qt-project.org and Qt 5 kind: event speakers: - lars_knoll - thiago_macieira slug: qtprojectorg day: Sun start: 11:40 end: 11:40 event_id: 612 ---

Track: CrossDesktop Devroom

The Qt project was established in October to drive further development of Qt as an open source project. The main development effort of qt-project.org goes into Qt 5, the next major version of Qt that is currently being developed. The talk will explain the setup of qt-project.org, and how people can participate and contribute to the project. It'll also give an overview over Qt 5.
