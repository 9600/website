--- title: dma_buf buffer sharing kind: event speakers: - daniel_vetter slug: dma_buf day: Sat start: 14:00 end: 14:00 event_id: 929 ---

Track: X.org+OpenICC Devroom

This talk covers the desing of dmabuf as merged into Linux Kernel 3.3 and deep-dives into some of the technical issues that make buffer sharing non-trivial. It will also explore what's definitely still missing and look at what crazy stuff still might come to dmabuf in future.
