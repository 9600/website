--- title: MINIX 3 and BSD kind: event speakers: - arun_thomas slug: minux3_and_bsd day: Sun start: 09:15 end: 09:15 event_id: 846 ---

MINIX 3 is a BSD-licensed operating system focused on dependability. To improve the user experience, the MINIX team has been working to provide a more BSD-like environment on MINIX 3. This talk will describe our current progress on this front and our plans for the future.
