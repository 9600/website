This short talk is intended as the opening talk of the entire devroom. After a welcome greeting, I will briefly ponder about the motivation for meeting together and attempt a basic quantitative comparison of participating projects based on available project-metric data. The goal of this quick comparison is to illustrate some apparent similarities and differences between our projects, and to further support our common cause.
