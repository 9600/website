Welcome to the Ada Developer Room at FOSDEM 2012, which is organized by Ada-Belgium in cooperation with Ada-Europe.

Ada-Belgium and Ada-Europe are both non-profit organizations set to promote the use of Ada, and to disseminate the knowledge of it into academic, research and industrial establishments in Belgium and Europe, resp. Ada-Europe has member-organizations, such as Ada-Belgium, in various countries.

More information on this event is available on the Ada-Belgium web-site (see link).
