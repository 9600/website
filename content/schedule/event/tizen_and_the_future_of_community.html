This session will provide a basic intro to Tizen and will talk about the community transition from MeeGo to Tizen. We'll do a quick recap from past year of the MeeGo community along with some things we learned along the way. The rest of the presentation will talk about what we are doing to form the Tizen community and how you can participate. I'll also gather feedback during the Q&A on what you would like to see us do differently in the Tizen community.
