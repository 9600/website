--- title: Infodesk moved to K building --- <% # vim: set ts=2 sw=2 et ai ft=eruby: %>

For years, the main FOSDEM Infodesk has been at the entrance of the H building. This year, we've decided to move it to the K building. We hope this will allow us to somewhat reduce the bottleneck of visitors on Saturday morning.

A small Infodesk presence will remain in the H building to help people find the information they are looking for.
