--- title: Call for Stands deadline: 2012-11-28 ---

We offer open source projects a place for a stand in the hallways.

Stands can be used to share information, demo software, sell merchandising, give away goodies and so forth. They allow projects to present themselves to the visitors in a more personal fashion.

What we offer:

  • one (max. two tables) of 1m80 x 80cm each, positioned in one of the three buildings with devrooms,
  • during the whole two days of the conference,
  • two chairs per table,
  • one power socket type C/E (bring connectors/adapters, etc... yourself),
  • fast uplink shared wireless Internet access (a and b),

Further practical considerations:

  • The proposal should be about a free or open source software project and/or community,
  • There is no cost or fee involved when having a stand,
  • The stand should be manned by at least 2 persons during the duration of the conference.
